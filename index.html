About us
We are an animal protection organization that supports dogs in Germany and in Bihac in Bosnia-Herzegovina. We support the local dog asylum, local education, castration and vaccination campaigns, as well as the placement and care of dogs in their forever homes. Usually dogs get adopted in Germany or Austria.

Together we promote the fight against animal misery and are on site at regular intervals. All club members work on a voluntary basis. Our association is financed exclusively through donations, 100 percent of which go to the dogs and are used for them. Some of the things we provide for our dogs with your donations are food, vaccinations, castrations, upragde of facilities, etc.

Donations

Unfortunately, animal welfare is one thing above all else – expensive. Luckily, there are many different ways you can support us - from one-off donations, sponsorships, membership fees, to food or material donations. And our protégés will thank you from the bottom of their hearts! All donations are also tax deductible because we are recognized as a non-profit organization by the tax office! Up to €200, a bank statement or transfer slip is sufficient; for amounts above that, we will issue you a donation receipt (if desired, also for smaller amounts, please just contact us). If you order dog food through Dinner for Dogs, we also receive a donation. Great thing, right?
